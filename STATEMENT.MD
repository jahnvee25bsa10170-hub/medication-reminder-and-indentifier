# Medication Reminder & Pill Identifier - Project Statement

## Problem Statement
#Many people struggle with medication management - forgetting to take pills on time and encountering unidentified medications in their household. This leads to missed doses, improper medication use, and potential health risks. There's a need for a simple, accessible tool that helps users remember their medication schedule and identify unknown pills quickly.

## Scope of the Project
#This project develops a desktop application that:
#- Stores and manages personal medication schedules
#- Provides timely reminders for medication intake
#- Identifies common pills based on physical characteristics
#- Maintains a local database of user medications

#The scope includes basic pill identification using a predefined database, time-based reminders, and local data storage. It does not include advanced image recognition, cloud synchronization, or medical professional features.

## Target Users
#- Elderly patients - who need help remembering complex medication schedules
#- People with chronic conditions - requiring regular medication management
#- Caregivers - managing medications for family members or patients
#- General users - who occasionally need to identify unknown pills found at home
#- Busy individuals - who frequently forget to take their medications on time

## High-level Features
#- Medication schedule creation and storage
#- Background reminder system with minute-by-minute checking
#- Pill identification using color, shape, and imprint codes
#- Simple text-based user interface
#- Persistent local data storage
#- Preloaded common medication database
#- Real-time notification alerts
